About Outbrain

Outbrain is a leading content discovery platform, serving more than 200 billion recommendations monthly, to more than 557 million users. We practice continuous integration and deployment, and release our services to thousands of servers, residing in a geographically dispersed, multi-datacenter environment, on a daily basis.
